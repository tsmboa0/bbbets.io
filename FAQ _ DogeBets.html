FAQ

How do referrals work?
  • Referrals give you an opportunity to profit by bringing in more players
  • For every player you bring, you will be rewarded with 1% of their future wagers. These rewards will accumulate under your wallet address within our smart contract and can be redeemed whenever you like and as often as you like.
  • In order for a player to become referred to you - just send them your referral link. The player then simply needs to bet one time after following your link; this permanently refers their wallet to yours.
  • Once they are referred to you, the track of referrals is kept fully on-chain
  • Your referrals are manageable through BSC referrals and DogeChain referrals pages
Why are there two referral pages?
  • Our referrals are based on-chain.
  • Because of that, there is a separate smart contract for each chain
Do I have separate referrals on each chain?
  • No. Our system is designed to seamlessly synchronize your referrals across chains
  • However, due to the on-chain implementation(user gets referred to you when they bet), synchronization of your referrals happens when they bet cross-chain. Here's an example:
  • User A is referred to you. They played a game of BNB prediction on BSC and became referred to you there. For them to become referred to you on DogeChain, they need to play any game there at least once, too.
  • Please note, that it is not necessary for you to send them your referral link again
  • Please also note that this is an experimental feature and things can go wrong. Report any issues you encounter in our Telegram community
How do I win?
  • Wager BNB on if the price of BNB-USDT will go UP or DOWN during the next round
  • Choose correctly and claim your prize
  • Prizes can be claimed from the Previous Card or the History page

What is a house bet?
  • DogeBets places a random sized bet on both sides (UP and DOWN) each round
  • This allows anyone to play 24/7, even if no one else is currently playing
  • House bets are locked in the contract each round and can be verified on-chain
What happens if a round ends in a tie?
  • The round is automatically cancelled and eligble for reclaim
  • House fees are not applied to reclaimed rounds
  • Check the History page or the Previous card for the 'refund' buttom
What is the house fee?
  • There is a 5% "DogeHouse" fee on all claimed winnings
  • The house fee only applies to winning rounds
How are the payout ratios calculated?
  • The payout ratios automatically update every time a bet is placed
  • Example: Total pool size = 2.5 BNB (1.0 BNB DOWN, 1.5 BNB UP)
    • UP payout = 2.5 / 1.5 = 1.66x
    • DOWN payout = 2.5 / 1.0 = 2.50x
Are there any keyboard shortcuts?
  • Yes, there are!
  • Backscape - works only on history page; takes you back to predictions page
  • C - chart/cards. Works only on the prediction game pages. Cycles between chart and cards
  • H - history. Works only on the prediction game pages.
